Welcome to Cutie Paws!

Welcome fellow Cat Friend! We are a non-profit organization that helps rehome cats and kittens. We work with all types of feline friends!

See our adoptable pets and submit an Adoption Request Form to adopt one of our babies.

We even help cats who:

  • are senior citizens (Age 7+)
  • have disabilities or impairments
  • have FIV
  • are bonded pairs and must stay together

Adoption Eligibility Checklist:

  1. Must be 18 years of age or older
  2. Must spend time with the cats and kittens to determine compatbility
  3. Must be able to pay $50 rehoming fee
  4. Must be available for one follow up visit.
