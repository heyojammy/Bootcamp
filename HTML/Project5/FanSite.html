What is a Cucco?

Cuccos are chicken-like creatures that are common in The Legend of Zelda series. They are similar to chickens and sound like a rooster. In most games, Cuccos can summon a flock of Cuccos to attack Link if he has hit them too many times.

Cuccos appear in:

  • Link's Awakening
  • Ocarina of Time
  • Majora's Mask
  • Oracle of Seasons and Oracle of Ages
  • Twilight Princess
  • Phantom Hourglass
  • A Link Between Worlds
  • Breath of the Wild
  • And more! They've even guest starred in Super Smash Bros
